Community Involvement

At Markelo, we believe in giving back to the community and making a positive impact in the lives of those around us. Our commitment to social responsibility is an integral part of our company values and culture.

Markelo Cares

Our flagship initiative, Markelo Cares, focuses on providing support to local communities in the areas of education, health, and environment. Through this program, we have funded scholarships, donated to local hospitals, and organized tree planting events.

Volunteer Days

Markelo encourages employees to contribute their time and skills to meaningful causes. We offer paid volunteer days for our employees, allowing them to engage with local non-profit organizations and make a difference in their communities.

Partnerships

We actively collaborate with various non-profit organizations and charities to create a positive impact on a larger scale. Our partnerships have helped us drive meaningful change in areas such as disaster relief, child welfare, and wildlife conservation.

Get Involved

We're always looking for new opportunities to make a difference. If you know of an organization or cause that would benefit from our support, please get in touch with us at community@markelo.com.
